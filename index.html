Present Emir of Tikau Emirate

ALHAJI ABUBAKAR MUHAMMADU IBN GREMA, The eldest of Alh. Dr. Muhammadu Abubakar Ibn Grema, the late Emir of Tikau. Alhaji Abubakar Muhammadu Ibn Grema has a Bachelor of Agric Economics & Extension, from Bayero University Kano,an MSc in Agric Economics (Applied Econometrics, Development and Resource Economics) and a PhD (inview) in Rural Development. He has worked in various capacities at Yobe state University. Before his appointment, Abubakar Muhammadu Ibn Gream, was the Principal Manager, at Yobe State University Farm.
